We are now moving beyond the Hackathon MVP into Version 2. The objective is NOT to add random features, but to evolve SBI EngageAI from a recommendation engine into a true AI Financial Copilot.

Do NOT start coding immediately. First understand the complete vision and generate an updated PRD v2. We will discuss and review it before implementation.

=========================================================
VISION
=========================================================

Today our application analyzes a customer and recommends SBI products.

Version 2 should instead maintain a living Financial Twin (Digital Financial Profile) of every customer.

Every time the customer updates their information, goals, assets, expenses, or family details, the AI should update this Financial Twin and all recommendations, graphs, scores, and plans should update automatically.

The AI should behave like a real SBI Relationship Manager.

It should understand the customer more deeply over time instead of asking everything once.
